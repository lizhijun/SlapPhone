Features

Everything You Need to Slap

SlapMac free packs powerful slap detection, a rich sound library, and customization into a lightweight macOS menu bar app.

🎯

Real-time Slap Detection

Uses Apple Silicon accelerometer at 100Hz to detect trackpad impacts instantly. Smart gravity filter eliminates false triggers.

🎵

11 Built-in Sound Effects

From hilarious fart sounds to dramatic punches. Choose your favorite from the dropdown and slap away.

🎤

Custom Sound Packs

Import your own MP3, WAV, AIFF, M4A audio files. Create unlimited custom sound packs with your favorite sounds.

⚡

Screen Flash Effect

Full-screen white flash on impact with intensity-mapped brightness. Toggle on or off from the menu bar.

🔌

USB Moaner Mode

Surprise your coworkers! Plays sounds when USB devices are plugged in or removed. Optional and toggleable.

🎮

Sound Selector

Pick exactly which sound plays on every slap. No randomness - full control from a convenient dropdown menu.
